================================================================================
1. PROJECT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

This project aims to protect 10,000 hectares of primary rainforest in the 
Amazon basin from planned deforestation activities. The project area consists
of mature tropical moist forest with high biodiversity value.

Project Location: Amazonas State, Brazil
Nearest City: Manaus
Total Area: 10,000 hectares

3. BASELINE CARBON STOCK
================================================================================

Year: 2024
Forest Type: Tropical Moist Forest (Primary)
Stratum: Single stratum - Dense forest canopy

Carbon Stock Calculation:
- Above-ground biomass: 350 tonnes/hectare
- Below-ground biomass: 70 tonnes/hectare
- Soil organic carbon: 80 tonnes/hectare

Total Carbon Stock: 180 tonnes CO2/hectare

NDVI Baseline: 0.82

(Note: This high NDVI value represents dense, healthy tropical forest canopy)

================================================================================
4. CLAIMED EMISSION REDUCTIONS
================================================================================

Baseline Scenario: Without the project, 500 hectares would be deforested 
annually for cattle ranching.

Project Scenario: Full protection of the forest area.

Annual Emission Reductions:
- Avoided deforestation: 500 hectares/year
- Average carbon stock: 180 tCO2e/ha
- Total annual credits: 90,000 tCO2e/year

Credit Period: 2024-2033 (10 years)
Total Expected Credits: 900,000 tCO2e

================================================================================
5. MONITORING METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Methodology: VM0015 - Avoided Unplanned Deforestation

Monitoring Approach:
- Sentinel-2 satellite imagery analysis (10m resolution)
- Annual ground truthing surveys
- Community patrol reports

Reference Period: 2020-2024

Quality Assurance:
- Cloud-free imagery required (<10% cloud cover)
- Minimum 4 observations per year
- Ground truthing sample: 5% of project area
